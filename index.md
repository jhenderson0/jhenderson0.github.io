## About Me

I am a PhD student in the Division of Infection and Immunity at University College London. I'm based in the Qimmuno lab and supervised by Dr Andreas Tiffeau-Mayer and Prof. Benny Chain. My research is highly interdisciplinary and I use techniques from theoretical physics, ecology, and statistical learning to help address open problems in immunology.

I'm currently looking for PostDoctoral research opportunities!
